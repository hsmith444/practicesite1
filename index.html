Synopsis

Throughout my career, I have enjoyed the challenge of researching complex topics and providing concise, impactful assessments that drive warfighter and policy-level decision-making. As a first assignment officer for the Air Force, I was assigned to be a Command Briefer and routinely gave briefings to general officers. At my second assignment, I served as the Chief of my branch, where I significantly increased the professionalism of the team briefing both aircrew and the group and wing staff. Not only was I known as an outstanding briefer, but I also mentored my subordinates and peers in formal and informal briefings. As the chief of the Plans and Policy Division, I diligently researched doctrine to incorporate into personnel recovery doctrine, giving me a comprehensive understanding of relevant processes within the community. I completed my master’s degree in October 2018. I am currently fulfilling a new role as my agency’s liaison to the community; however, I am looking to make a career transition to be a writer and editor.

Skillsets

Communication/Writing. Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate both orally and written. Often sought out for writing and editing ability, experienced in analytical writing and research, and often voluntarily edits and assists junior analysts. Selected for focused research project on space policy by Air University for the Air Command and Staff College Online Master’s Degree Course. As a Staff Officer, routinely edited documents to ensure adherence to writing standards as well as maintaining meaning of the original author.

Analysis and Research. Over fifteen years developing and demonstrating in-depth capabilities for analysis and research. Demonstrated ability to conduct independent and complex research projects for analysis and for policy program review. Relied on knowledge of writing standards to convey analysis. Experience conducting policy review of multiple agency-wide programs.

Collaboration. Collaboration has been key throughout career, partnering with many partners. Experience in collaborating written products with different partners.
