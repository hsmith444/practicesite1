Career Options

Having served in the Department of  Defense for over twenty years, I am now exploring my career options. I would like to make a transition from my current position as a liaison to a writer/editor. I would also like to transition to a different department in the U.S. government, hopefully the Department of the Interior or Agriculture. That being said, I still enjoy the study of warfare. If I continue my career in the Department of Defense, I'd like to move on to a position where I contribute to the study of warfare and international humanitarian law.
